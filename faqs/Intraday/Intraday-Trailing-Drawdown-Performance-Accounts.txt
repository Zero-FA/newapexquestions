The Intraday Performance Account (PA) is a Simulated Funded (Sim Funded) account awarded after you pass the Intraday Evaluation. It is issued in the same size as the Evaluation account you passed and follows the same Intraday Trailing Drawdown structure.

Trading Rules
1. Do Not Breach the Intraday Trailing Drawdown
Your account balance, including unrealized PnL, may never touch or fall below the Intraday Trailing Drawdown threshold. If it does, all positions are automatically liquidated and the PA is permanently closed.

Frequently Asked Questions
How many PAs can I have?
You may hold up to 20 PAs active at the same time. This cap applies across all PA accounts combined, whether different sizes Legacy, EOD, or Intraday Drawdown.

Is the Performance Account a live account?
No, the Performance Account is a Simulated Funded (Sim Funded) account.

Does hitting the Daily Loss Limit close my PA?
No. Hitting the DLL pauses trading for the remainder of the session. The account remains active and resets at the next session open.

Can my position size increase?
Yes. Position size increases as you move up Scaling Levels based on your End-of-Day balance, up to the account’s maximum level.

What happens if I violate the Hedging Rule?
Violation of the Hedging & Correlated Instruments Rule results in immediate Performance Account closure.

Can my account be closed for inactivity?
Yes. If you do not record at least one $150 net profit day within 150 consecutive calendar days, the account will be closed.

Do I keep 100% of approved payouts?
Yes. Approved payouts are issued at a 100% payout split once payout eligibility requirements are met.
