Intraday Trailing Drawdown Performance Accounts (PA)

Overview
The Intraday Performance Account (PA) is a Simulated Funded account awarded after passing the Intraday Evaluation.

The Performance Account is issued in the same size as the Evaluation that was passed and follows the same Intraday Trailing Drawdown structure.


Intraday Trailing PA at a Glance

Key characteristics:

- Intraday trailing drawdown enforced in real time
- Trailing stops once Max Trailing Drawdown Amount + $100 is reached
- Daily Loss Limit (DLL) enforced intraday
- Tier-based scaling structure
- 100% payout split after meeting payout eligibility requirements

Trading Rules

Rule 1 — Do Not Breach the Intraday Trailing Drawdown
Your account balance, including unrealized PnL, may never touch or fall below the Intraday Trailing Drawdown threshold.

If the threshold is reached:

- All open positions are automatically liquidated
- The Performance Account is permanently closed

See: Intraday Trailing Drawdown Explained


Rule 2 — No Prohibited Trading Activity
All trading activity must follow Apex Trader Funding risk and conduct guidelines.

Violating the rules, including hedging violations or attempts to circumvent trading policies, will result in immediate account closure.

See: Prohibited Activities


Frequently Asked Questions

How many Performance Accounts can I have?
You may hold up to 20 Performance Accounts at the same time.

This limit applies across all Performance Accounts combined, including different account sizes, Legacy accounts, End-of-Day (EOD) accounts, and Intraday Drawdown accounts.


Is the Performance Account a live account?
No. The Performance Account is a Simulated Funded (Sim Funded) trading account.


Does hitting the Daily Loss Limit close my Performance Account?
No. Hitting the Daily Loss Limit pauses trading for the remainder of the trading session.

The account remains active and trading resumes at the next session open.


Can my position size increase?
Yes. Position size increases as you progress through scaling tiers based on your End-of-Day balance, up to the maximum contract level allowed for the account.


What happens if I violate the Hedging Rule?
Violation of the Hedging and Correlated Instruments Rule results in immediate closure of the Performance Account.


Can my account be closed for inactivity?
Yes. If the account does not record at least one trading day with $150 or more in net profit within 150 consecutive calendar days, the account will be closed due to inactivity.


Do I keep 100% of approved payouts?
Yes. Approved payouts are issued at a 100% payout split once payout eligibility requirements are met.
