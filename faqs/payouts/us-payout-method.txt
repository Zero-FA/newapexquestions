Payout Method: US-Based Users

Overview
If you live in the United States, Apex Trader Funding sends payouts via ACH direct deposit to your US-based bank account.

Important:
You must use your ACH routing number, not a wire transfer routing number. Using the wrong routing number may cause payout delays or rejection.


What You Will Need

Before setting up your payout method, make sure you have the following information:

• A US-based checking or savings account
• Your ACH routing number (not a wire routing number)
• Your bank account number
• Your Social Security Number (SSN) or Tax Identification Number (TIN)


Step-by-Step Setup Instructions

Step 1 – Log Into Your Apex Dashboard

Go to:
https://dashboard.apextraderfunding.com/login

Log in using your Apex Trader Funding account credentials.


Step 2 – Open the Payout Method Section

After logging in:

• Locate the "Payout Method" tab on the left-side menu
• Click the tab to open the payout setup window

Scroll to the bottom of the information window and click:

CHANGE PAYOUT METHOD HERE


Step 3 – Enter Your Personal Information

Complete the following fields:

US Citizen or US Taxpayer
Select: YES

Social Security Number / Tax ID
Enter your SSN or Tax Identification Number

Payout Type
Select: Direct Deposit

Account Type
Choose either:
• Checking
• Savings

Name Fields
Enter your legal First and Last name exactly as it appears on your Apex Profile.


Step 4 – Enter Your Bank Information

Fill out the following fields carefully:

• Bank Name
• ACH Routing Number
• Bank Account Number

Important:
Make sure the routing number you enter is the ACH routing number, not the wire routing number. Many banks have different numbers for ACH transfers and wire transfers.

Entering the wrong routing number will cause payout delays or rejection.


Step 5 – Save Your Information

Once all fields are entered correctly:

• Review the information carefully
• Click Save

Your payout method is now set up.

You will be able to request payouts once your Performance Account becomes eligible.



Business Account Payouts

If your Apex account is registered as a business and you want payouts sent to a business bank account, enter your details as follows:

Social Security Number / Tax ID
Enter your business Employer Identification Number (EIN)

First Name Field
Enter your full business name
Example:
ABC Business LLC

Last Name Field
This field will automatically populate and cannot be changed.

Important:
Avoid using special characters or symbols in the company name, as they may cause payout processing issues.



Why Can't I Edit the Last Name Field?

The Last Name field is automatically populated and cannot be edited.

If your Apex account was registered as a business (the business option was selected during sign-up), the system already recognizes the account as a business account even if a personal name appears.

To receive payouts under your business:

• Enter your EIN or Tax ID
• Connect a matching business bank account



Bank Location Requirements

Your bank account must be located in the United States.

Additionally, your Apex account must list the United States as your country of residence.

If your residency is listed outside the United States, you cannot use a US-based bank account to receive payouts.
