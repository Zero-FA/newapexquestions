Payout Method Information

Overview
Apex Trader Funding provides a simple payout system for both US-based and international traders. This guide explains how to set up your payout method, submit payout requests, and understand the payout processing timeline.


Setting Up Your Payout Method

Before requesting a payout, you must set up your payout method in your Apex Trader Funding dashboard.

The setup process depends on whether you are located inside or outside the United States.


US-Based Users

If you live in the United States, payouts are sent via ACH direct deposit to your US bank account.

Important:

• Use your ACH routing number
• Do not use a wire transfer routing number

Using the wrong routing number may delay your payout.

Full instructions for US payout setup are available on the US-based User Payout Method Setup page.


International Users

If you live outside the United States, payouts are processed through Plane, Apex Trader Funding’s international payment partner.

After your first payout request is approved:

• You will receive an email from Plane
• The email will contain instructions to link your local bank account

Be sure to check your spam or junk folder if the email does not arrive within a few days.

Full instructions are available on the International User Payment Method Setup page.



Submitting a Payout Request

To request a payout:

1. Log into your Apex Trader Funding dashboard
2. Open the Payouts tab
3. Select Request Payout

Any Performance Accounts (PAs) that are eligible for payout will appear in this section.


Payout Request Trading Requirements

After requesting a payout, you become eligible for your next payout request once you complete:

• Minimum 8 trading days
• At least 5 of those days must have $50 or more in profit
• No rule violations

These trading days can be viewed in the PA Charts.

The day count begins:

• The day after your last payout request
• Or the start of the account if no payout has been requested previously.


Trading After Submitting a Payout Request

You can continue trading immediately after submitting a payout request.

You do not need to wait for payout approval.

However, you should trade as if the payout amount has already been removed from your account balance.

This ensures your account remains above the required payout thresholds.


Important Balance Rule

If your account balance falls below the minimum payout threshold after submitting a payout request:

• The payout request will automatically be denied

You do not need to cancel or modify the request.

Simply continue trading until eligibility requirements are met again.



International Users — First Payout

For your first payout:

After approval, you will receive an email invitation from Plane.

Follow the instructions in that email to:

• Create your Plane account
• Connect your local bank account

Check your spam or junk folder if you do not receive the email within a few days.



Receiving Your Funds

Payout Timeline Overview

After submitting a payout request, the payout process typically follows this timeline:

1. Apex reviews your payout request within 2 business days.
2. If approved, the payout is sent within 3–4 business days.
3. Your bank or payment provider processes the deposit within 3–7 business days.

Most users receive their payout within approximately:

5 to 11 business days total.



How Funds Are Delivered

US Users
Payouts are delivered through ACH direct deposit to your US bank account.

International Users
Payouts are delivered through Plane and deposited into your linked local bank account.

Processing time may vary depending on your local bank.



Dashboard Status Shows "PAID"

If your Apex dashboard shows the payout status as:

PAID

but you have not yet received your funds:

Please allow up to an additional 7 business days for your bank or payment provider to process the deposit.

Do not submit a Help Desk ticket during this standard processing window.
