Payout Method: International Users

Overview
If you live outside the United States, Apex Trader Funding sends payouts through Plane, a secure international payment platform.

Once your first payout request is approved, you will receive an email invitation from Plane to connect your local bank account.

If you do not see the email, check your spam or junk folder.


What You Need Before Starting

Before setting up your payout method, make sure you have the following:

• A local bank account in your country of residence  
• A valid government-issued ID or passport  
• The email address used to log into your Apex Trader Funding dashboard


Step-by-Step Setup Instructions


Step 1 — Log Into Your Apex Dashboard

Go to:

https://dashboard.apextraderfunding.com/login

Log in using your Apex account credentials.


Step 2 — Open the Payout Method Page

From the left-hand dashboard menu, click:

Payout Method

A popup window will appear with instructions.

Scroll to the bottom of the page and click:

"CHANGE PAYOUT METHOD HERE"


Step 3 — Enter Required Personal Information

Fill out the payout method form with the following details:

US Citizen or US Taxpayer  
Select: No

Government ID Number  
Enter your Government ID or Passport number.

Payout Type  
Select: International

Name Fields  
Enter your legal first and last name exactly as shown on your Apex profile.

Important:
Avoid using special characters or symbols in your name, as this may prevent successful payment processing.

Email Address  
Enter the same email address used to log into your Apex dashboard.


Step 4 — Save Your Information

After confirming all information is correct, click:

Save

This completes your payout method setup.


Plane Account Invitation

After your first payout request is approved:

You will receive an email invitation from Plane within 1 to 4 business days.

Use the link in that email to:

• Create your Plane account
• Link your local bank account

Once your Plane account is active and linked to your bank, payouts can be processed.


Missing Plane Email

If you do not receive the Plane invitation email:

• Check your spam or junk folder.

Do not submit a Help Desk ticket unless more than:

5 business days

have passed without receiving the email.


Bank Account Requirements

Your bank account must be located in the same country as your declared residency.

Example:

If you live in Spain, your bank account must also be located in Spain.

If the bank country does not match your declared residency, the payout request will be rejected.

Update your payout method information if necessary before submitting payout requests.


Tax Information for International Users

Apex Trader Funding does not issue tax forms to non-US traders.

You are responsible for reporting your earnings according to the tax laws in your country of residence.

Important:

Apex Trader Funding cannot provide tax advice.

If you have questions about taxes, consult a qualified tax advisor.

The Apex Help Desk cannot assist with tax-related questions.
