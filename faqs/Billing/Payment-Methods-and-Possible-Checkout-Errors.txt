Payment Methods and Possible Checkout Errors

Overview
Apex Trader Funding supports specific payment methods for purchases and account fees. Payments must be made using a card that is registered under your own name.

Cards belonging to another person are not allowed and the transaction may be declined if the cardholder name does not match the purchaser.


Accepted Payment Methods
Apex Trader Funding accepts the following credit and debit cards:

Visa
Mastercard
Discover

Both credit and debit versions of these cards are supported as long as the card is issued in your name.


Unsupported Payment Methods
The following payment methods are not accepted:

Gift cards
American Express cards
Cryptocurrency


Payment Name Requirement
All purchases must be made using a card registered in your own name.

Payments made with cards belonging to another person are not permitted and may be declined or blocked.


Possible Checkout Errors
Payment failures can occur for several reasons including bank security restrictions, incorrect billing information, or retrying the payment too quickly.

Below are the most common checkout issues and how to resolve them.


Error 609 — Transaction Blocked by Issuer
Error 609 typically means that your bank or card issuer has blocked the transaction for security or fraud-prevention reasons.

How to resolve this:

Contact your bank or card issuer and ask them to approve or authorize the transaction.

Confirm that your card is allowed to process:

Online transactions
International transactions (some banks block these by default)


Billing Information Errors
Even small mismatches in billing information can cause a payment to fail.

Make sure the following details match exactly what appears on your bank statement:

Full name
Billing address
Country
ZIP or postal code
Card number
Expiration date
CVV security code


Retrying the Payment
Once your bank confirms the transaction is authorized, you can retry the payment through your Apex Dashboard.

If the payment continues to fail after contacting your bank, try using a different supported card to avoid further delays.


Duplicate Payment Errors
Duplicate payment errors usually occur when the same payment is attempted repeatedly within a short time.

This typically happens when:

You attempt another payment immediately after a failed transaction
Multiple retries are submitted within a short period of time


How to Prevent Duplicate Payment Errors
To avoid duplicate payment errors:

Wait 2–5 minutes before attempting the payment again
Refresh your Apex Dashboard before retrying
Do not click the checkout button multiple times during the same payment attempt
