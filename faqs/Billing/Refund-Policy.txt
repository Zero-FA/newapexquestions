Refund Policy

Overview
Apex Trader Funding does not provide refunds for purchases made through the website. This applies to initial account purchases and optional services. All purchases should be reviewed carefully before completing checkout.


General Purchases
Refunds are not offered for any purchases made through the Apex Trader Funding website.

This includes:

Initial account purchases
Optional services
Add-ons or additional account features


Non-Refundable Fees and Add-Ons
Certain optional services are also non-refundable. These include:

PA Activation Fees
Charged when transitioning from an Evaluation account to a Performance Account.

Data Fees
Includes services such as Market Depth or additional session logins.

Before purchasing these services, make sure they fit your trading needs to avoid unnecessary expenses.


Technology Issues
Refunds are not issued for issues related to trading platforms or technical errors.

Examples include:

Platform errors such as delayed order execution
Data feed errors
Market depth errors
Orders becoming stuck or not closing properly

These issues typically relate to third-party platforms that Apex Trader Funding does not control.

If you experience these problems, contact the third-party platform provider for assistance.


Incorrect Plan Selection
If you purchase the wrong plan or service, refunds are not permitted.

Available options include:

Continue using the purchased plan until it expires
Purchase the correct plan separately


Promotions and Coupon Codes
Refunds or adjustments are not issued for promotion or coupon code issues.

Examples include:

Entering a promo code incorrectly
Forgetting to apply a coupon during checkout
Purchasing outside the valid promotion period
Promotions that have already expired

Promotional discounts cannot be applied retroactively.


Refund Policy Summary

Initial Account Fee
Refund eligibility: No refunds.

Optional Add-Ons
Refund eligibility: No refunds for services such as Market Depth or additional session logins.

Incorrect Purchases or Technology Issues
Refund eligibility: No refunds for selecting the wrong plan, platform errors, data feed issues, or stuck orders.


How to Avoid Unwanted Charges
To prevent purchasing the wrong product or service:

Review the FAQ and product descriptions carefully before purchasing.

Contact the Apex Help Desk if you need clarification before completing a transaction.

Double-check the plan or service you are selecting during checkout to confirm it is correct.
