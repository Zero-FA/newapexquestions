Coupon Codes
How to Apply a Coupon
Coupons must be applied during checkout when making your purchase.
Before completing the transaction on the checkout page, double-check that the discounted price is correctly applied to your order. This cannot be amended after the payment is complete.
Missed Coupons or Discounts
Important Reminder: Once the payment is processed, coupon codes cannot be applied retroactively, and refunds or adjustments for missed discounts will not be provided.
To ensure you don’t miss out, review your order summary carefully before completing your purchase.
How to Add a Coupon Code
On the checkout page, enter the coupon code you were given, or "SAVENOW" if you don't have one.  You will need to agree to our Trading Rules and policies before you click "Next."
Once on the checkout page, review the discount amount and Terms, then fill out your credit card information to complete the purchase.
