PA Activation Process & Deadline Explained

Overview
Passing an Evaluation gives you the opportunity to activate a Performance Account (PA) of the same size and type as the Evaluation you passed.

Examples:
- Passing a 50K EOD Evaluation allows you to activate a 50K EOD Performance Account.
- Passing a 50K Live Intraday Trailing Evaluation allows you to activate a 50K Live Intraday Trailing Performance Account.


After Passing the Evaluation
Once you reach the profit target and satisfy all Evaluation requirements:

- Your account is reviewed at market close (4:59:59 PM ET).
- If the profit target is confirmed, the Evaluation is marked as Passed.
- The account will appear in the "Passed Evals" section of your dashboard after 6 PM ET.

Once the Evaluation is marked as Passed, it becomes eligible for Performance Account activation.


Activation Window
You have 7 calendar days to complete the PA Activation Fee.

Important details:

- The 7-day window begins when the Evaluation is officially marked as Passed after market close.
- The activation window does NOT start when the profit target is reached during the trading session.
- The activation opportunity expires permanently if the activation fee is not completed within the 7-day window.


Activation Timeline Example
Example scenario:

- The profit target is reached at 9:00 PM ET on Tuesday the 19th (which is part of Wednesday’s trading session).
- The Evaluation is not marked as Passed until after the market closes the following day.
- In this example, the Evaluation would be marked as Passed on Wednesday the 20th after 6 PM ET.
- The 7-day activation window begins on Wednesday the 20th.
- The final deadline to activate the PA would be Wednesday the 27th at 11:59 PM ET.


Passing on the Final Day
If your Evaluation is marked as Passed on the final day of your 30-day Evaluation access period:

- You will still receive the full 7 calendar days to activate your PA.
- The original Evaluation account itself may expire, but the activation window remains valid.


Activation Expiration
If the PA activation fee is not completed before midnight on the 7th calendar day after the Evaluation is marked as Passed:

- The activation opportunity expires permanently.
- The activation cannot be reinstated.
- To qualify again, you must pass a new Evaluation.


Notification
An email notification may be sent once your Evaluation is marked as Passed.

This email is informational only. Activation is completed directly through your dashboard.


How to Activate Your Performance Account
To activate your Performance Account:

1. Go to the "Passed Evals" section of your dashboard.
2. Click the "Purchase PA" link.
3. Complete the one-time PA Activation Fee.


After Payment
After the activation fee is completed:

- The Performance Account will be created using the same login credentials as the Evaluation.
- Account creation may take up to 6 hours.
- The automated account creation process begins after market close on the day you pass.

Please allow the full processing window before contacting Support.

If the Purchase PA link is not visible after two full calendar days, you may submit a support ticket.


PA Activation Fees (All Platforms)
The PA Activation Fee is a one-time payment per account.

Important details:

- The activation fee is not a subscription.
- The fee does not renew.
- The activation fee applies only to the specific Performance Account being activated.
- The activation fee cannot be transferred, reassigned, refunded, or reversed.

Once activated, the Performance Account remains valid as long as the account stays active.

If a Performance Account is disqualified, liquidated, or closed due to inactivity:

- The account is permanently closed.
- Market data access is revoked.
- A new Evaluation must be passed to receive another Performance Account.


Frequently Asked Questions

What happens if I do not activate my PA within 7 days?
If the activation fee is not completed within 7 calendar days after the Evaluation is marked as Passed, the activation link expires permanently. The opportunity cannot be reinstated. You must pass a new Evaluation to qualify again.


Does the 7-day window start when I hit the profit target?
No. The activation window begins when the Evaluation is officially marked as Passed after market close (after 6 PM ET), not when the profit target is reached during trading.


What if I pass on the last day of my Evaluation?
You will still receive the full 7 calendar days to activate your Performance Account even if the original Evaluation access period expires. The passed Evaluation may not appear in the trading platform but will remain visible in your dashboard.


Is the PA Activation Fee a subscription?
No. The activation fee is a one-time payment per account. It does not renew monthly and does not require cancellation.


Is the PA Activation Fee refundable?
No. The activation fee is a one-time payment and is not refundable, transferable, or reversible.


Can I transfer my activation to another account size?
No. The activation applies only to the specific Evaluation that was passed and must match the size and type of that Evaluation.


What happens after I pay the activation fee?
Your Performance Account will be created and will use the same login credentials as your Evaluation. Account creation may take up to 6 hours.


I paid but do not see my PA. What should I do?
Please allow up to 6 hours for account creation. Contact Support only if the account has not appeared after the full processing window.

Accounts activated after Friday market close will not be created until Sunday at 6 PM ET.


I passed but do not see the Purchase PA link.
The conversion process begins after market reopens at 6 PM ET on the day you pass.

If the Purchase PA link is still missing after two full calendar days, you may submit a support ticket.


Can I extend the 7-day activation period?
No. The activation window cannot be extended under any circumstances.


If my PA is later disqualified, do I need to pay again?
Yes. If a Performance Account is disqualified, liquidated, or closed, you must pass a new Evaluation and pay a new activation fee to receive another Performance Account.
