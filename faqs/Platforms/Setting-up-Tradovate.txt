Setting Up Tradovate

Overview
This guide explains how to log into Tradovate and access your Apex Trader Funding accounts after purchasing a Tradovate evaluation or Performance Account.

You must use a desktop web browser for the initial login. Do not use the mobile app for the first setup.


Initial Login

Step 1 — Go to the Tradovate Welcome Page
After purchasing your Tradovate account and receiving your login credentials, open the Tradovate welcome page in a desktop browser.

Sign in using your Tradovate username and password.


Account Creation Timing

Account creation, resets, and Performance Account activation may take longer during periods of high demand or market volatility.

Typical processing time:

Up to 6 hours after ordering your account.

If your account has not appeared after 6 hours, submit a help desk ticket.


Market Data Activation

After signing the market data agreements, it may take between:

10 to 90 minutes

for your live data to activate.

Important:
Do not submit a help desk ticket regarding missing live data until at least 90 minutes have passed after signing the data agreements.


Login Credential Requirements

When logging in to Tradovate:

Ensure your username and password do not contain spaces.

This includes:

- No spaces before the username
- No spaces after the username
- No spaces before or after the password


Account Setup

Step 2 — Complete Required Information

Fill out the required information fields.

Review and sign the required documents.


Step 3 — Select Data Classification

When prompted for data classification, select:

NON PROFESSIONAL


Accessing Your Tradovate Account

Step 4 — Locate Your Accounts

After completing setup, your Apex accounts will appear under the:

Simulation section

Click Launch to open the account.


Tradovate Dashboard

After launching the account, you will see the Tradovate trading dashboard.

This dashboard displays:

- Your trading account
- Order panels
- Charting tools
- Position information


Tradovate Platform Help

For detailed instructions on configuring the Tradovate dashboard, charts, and workspace settings, visit the Tradovate support page.


Trading on Tradovate

Once market data is activated, you can begin trading directly from the Tradovate web platform:

https://trader.tradovate.com
