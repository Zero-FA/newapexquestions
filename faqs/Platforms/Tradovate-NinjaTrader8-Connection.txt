Tradovate x NinjaTrader8 Connection Guide

Overview
This guide explains how to install NinjaTrader and connect it to your Tradovate account when trading with Apex Trader Funding.


Creating a NinjaTrader Account

Step 1 — Visit NinjaTrader Website
Go to:
https://ninjatrader.com

Step 2 — Open an Account
Click the "Open Account" button.

Step 3 — Enter Email Address
Enter your email address and submit the form.

NinjaTrader will send an activation link to your email.


Account Activation

Step 4 — Activate Your Account
Open the email from NinjaTrader and click the activation link.

Important Note:
If you receive a message that your email is already in use, sign up again using a different email address in order to access the risk-free trial and NinjaTrader dashboard.



Downloading NinjaTrader 8.1

Step 5 — Access the Risk-Free Trial
After logging in to your NinjaTrader dashboard, click:

Try Risk-Free Simulated Trading

Step 6 — Complete the Form
Fill out the required information.

If prompted to choose between Desktop or Web versions, you may close that dialog box.

Step 7 — Download NinjaTrader
Scroll to the bottom left of the dashboard and click Download.

Always download the most recent release of NinjaTrader.



Configuring NinjaTrader Settings

Step 8 — Open Account Settings
Click your profile icon in the top right corner of the NinjaTrader dashboard.

Select:
Settings

Step 9 — Configure Symbology Display Style
Navigate to:

Preferences → Symbology Display Style

Change the display format to:

Numeric

Save the changes.



Installing NinjaTrader

Step 10 — Run Installer
Open the installer file you downloaded earlier.

Follow the installation instructions until NinjaTrader finishes installing.

Step 11 — Launch NinjaTrader
Open the NinjaTrader desktop application.



Logging Into NinjaTrader

Step 12 — Enter Credentials
Log in using the NinjaTrader username and password you created during account setup.

Important:
You must enter these credentials every time you start NinjaTrader.



Selecting Simulation Mode

Step 13 — Choose Simulation
If prompted to choose between Live or Simulation mode, select:

Simulation

Apex Trader Funding accounts operate only in simulation mode.



Enable Multi-Provider Mode

Step 14 — Enable Multi-Provider
Inside NinjaTrader Control Center:

Go to:
Tools → Options → General

Enable the checkbox labeled:

Multi-provider

Click Apply and OK.

Restart NinjaTrader to apply the setting.



Connecting Tradovate to NinjaTrader

Step 15 — Configure Connection
After restarting NinjaTrader:

Go to:
Connections → Configure

In the Available connections list, select:

NinjaTrader

Click Add.


Step 16 — Enter Account Credentials

In the Properties section:

Username:
Enter your Tradovate username from the Apex Trader Funding dashboard.

Password:
Enter your Tradovate password.

Click Apply, then OK.



Establishing the Connection

Step 17 — Connect to Account
Go to:

Connections

Select the connection you created (example: My NinjaTrader).

Your Tradovate accounts should now load in NinjaTrader.



Common Error Fix


Error Message:
“Input string was not in a correct format.”


Cause
The Symbology Display Style is not set correctly.


Solution

Open NinjaTrader Settings.

Go to:
Preferences → Symbology Display Style

Change the format to:

Numeric

Save the changes and restart NinjaTrader.



Troubleshooting

If problems continue after following this guide:

Contact Apex Trader Funding support.

Or contact the platform support teams for:

NinjaTrader
Tradovate



Conclusion

After completing these steps, NinjaTrader 8.1 should be successfully installed and connected to your Tradovate account.

You can now trade Apex Trader Funding accounts using NinjaTrader in simulation mode.
