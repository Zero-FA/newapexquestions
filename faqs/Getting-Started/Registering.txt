Registering as a Person or Business

Overview
Apex Trader Funding offers two main types of user accounts:

Personal User Accounts
Business User Accounts

These accounts allow access to the Apex dashboard where traders manage evaluations, Performance Accounts (PA), affiliate tools, and account settings.


Personal User Accounts

What is a Personal User Account?
A Personal User Account is created when a user signs up to purchase an evaluation subscription or join the affiliate program.

This account provides access to the Apex dashboard where you can:

- Manage your trading accounts
- Purchase evaluations
- Activate Performance Accounts
- Access affiliate links and resources
- View account performance data


One Personal Account Rule
Each individual may create only ONE Personal User Account.

Creating multiple personal accounts is a violation of Apex Trader Funding policy.

If additional personal accounts are discovered:

- Those accounts will be closed
- Any profits or affiliate commissions from those accounts may be forfeited


Business User Accounts

Creating a Business Account
Traders who wish to trade through a business entity may create a Business User Account.

A Business User Account requires:

- A valid EIN
- Possible additional documentation verifying the business


Allowed Business Structures
Business accounts must be solely owned and controlled by the trader.

Eligible U.S. business entities include:

- Sole Proprietorships
- Single-Member LLCs
- S Corporations with a single majority shareholder (80% or more ownership)

Similar international business structures may also qualify.


Ownership Requirement
The trader must be the sole owner and operator of the business entity.

No additional members, partners, employees, or shareholders may trade on behalf of the business.

Verification documents may include:

- Operating Agreement
- Articles of Organization
- Other official ownership documentation


User Account Limits
Each person may have:

- ONE Personal User Account
- ONE Business User Account

Creating additional accounts beyond these limits violates Apex Trader Funding policies.


Account Transfer Rules
User accounts cannot be transferred from one Apex login to another Apex login.

Account names and ownership details cannot be changed after registration.


Business Account Registration Rules

Registering as a Business
If you intend to trade under a business entity such as an LLC, you must select the business option when signing up for your evaluation.

A Personal User Account cannot later be converted into a Business User Account.


Payment Rules
Personal accounts must use personal bank accounts for payments and payouts.

Business accounts must use business payment methods.

Personal accounts cannot be paid out to business bank accounts, and business accounts cannot be paid out to personal bank accounts.


How to Register as a Business

To create a Business User Account:

1. Click the Sign Up button on the Apex Trader Funding homepage
2. Select the account size and trading platform
3. On the sign-up page, check the box:
   "Is this user account going to be owned by a company?"
4. Enter the Business or Company Name
5. Under Designated Trader Information, enter your legal name exactly as it appears on your government-issued ID
6. Use your business address and phone number
7. Use your business credit card for evaluation purchases and PA activation fees


Designated Trader Requirement
The designated trader must be the majority owner of the company.

This means the trader must own at least 80% of the business entity.


Account Restriction Rules

Bypassing Restrictions
Creating additional user accounts to bypass account restrictions is strictly prohibited.

This includes attempts to bypass:

- Probation restrictions
- Evaluation restrictions
- Special trading supervision


Household Account Restrictions
Creating additional user accounts within the same household to bypass restrictions is also prohibited.

Violations may result in termination of all connected user accounts.


Performance Account Limits

Maximum PA Limit
The maximum number of Performance Accounts allowed per user is 20.

This limit applies across both personal and business accounts combined.

Example:
You cannot have 20 PAs under your personal account and 20 under your business account.


Professional Data Status

Professional Classification
Certain traders or businesses may be classified as professional data users.

Professional traders may be required to pay additional exchange fees.

Example:
CME professional data fees may be at least $115 per month.

Most traders and LLCs are typically classified as non-professional traders.


Account Ownership Policy

Single User Requirement
All Apex accounts must be single-user accounts.

For corporate accounts:

- The majority shareholder must be the registered trader
- No other employees or partners may trade on the account


Ownership Changes
If ownership of the company changes and the registered trader no longer owns at least 80% of the business:

- The account will be considered in violation of policy
- The account may be immediately forfeited without refund


Shared Accounts
Shared accounts, multiple-user access, and third-party trading are strictly prohibited.


Eligible Organizations

Who Can Open an Account
Apex accounts are available to:

- Individual traders
- For-profit business entities


Ineligible Organizations
The following organizations cannot open Apex accounts:

- Churches
- Charities
- Non-profit organizations
- Tax-exempt organizations

These organizations operate under different legal and financial rules that are not compatible with Apex account verification requirements.
